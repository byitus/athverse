Aasi Multi Speciality Hospital is a 100-bedded healthcare facility in Ramanathapuram dedicated to providing affordable and quality medical services to every section of society. Founded by Dr. Asik Ameen — an experienced Orthopedic Surgeon — the hospital is built on patient-centered care and genuine compassion.
